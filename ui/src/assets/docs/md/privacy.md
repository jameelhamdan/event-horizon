1. Who we are

eventhorizonai.dev is an independent open-source project that provides a real-time map of global events sourced from publicly available news feeds. We do not operate as a legal entity and do not sell products or services. For questions, contact [contact@eventhorizonai.dev](mailto:contact@eventhorizonai.dev).

2. What data we collect

We collect as little data as possible. Here is what our infrastructure processes:

- **Server access logs** — your IP address, browser User-Agent, and requested URL are logged by our web server. These logs are used solely for security monitoring and are retained for a maximum of 30 days before automatic deletion.
- **Analytics cookies (optional)** — if you accept analytics, Google Analytics 4 is loaded. This sets cookies that allow Google to measure traffic, page views, and aggregate usage patterns. See Section 4 for details.

We do not operate user accounts, collect names, email addresses, or any other directly identifying personal data through the website itself.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

- **Server logs** — processed under _Legitimate Interest_ (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for security and abuse prevention. Retained for 30 days.
- **Analytics cookies** — processed under your explicit _Consent_ (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). Analytics only load after you click "Accept cookies" in the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 6).

4. Cookies

We use **one category of cookies**:

- **Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4)** — only set if you accept. GA4 sets cookies including \`_ga\`, \`\_ga_\*\` with a 2-year lifetime. These are used to distinguish users and sessions and to measure aggregate site usage. Google processes this data under their own [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). No advertising or remarketing features are enabled.

We do not use any essential, functional, or preference cookies. The site works identically with or without analytics cookies accepted.

5. Third-party data processors

- **Google LLC (Analytics)** — analytics data, if consented, is processed by Google in the United States under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Hosting provider** — our server infrastructure provider processes server logs as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with any other third parties.

6. Your rights and cookie withdrawal

Under GDPR you have the right to:

- Access, rectify, or erase personal data we hold about you
- Object to or restrict processing
- Data portability
- Log a complaint with your national supervisory authority

**To withdraw analytics consent**, open your browser's developer tools, go to Application → Local Storage → eventhorizonai.dev, and delete the \`eh_cookie_consent\` key. Then reload the page and choose "Decline" in the cookie banner. You can also clear all cookies for this site in your browser settings.

For any data requests, contact us at [contact@eventhorizonai.dev](mailto:contact@eventhorizonai.dev). We aim to respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

- Server access logs: 30 days
- GA4 analytics data: 14 months (Google's default retention)

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
